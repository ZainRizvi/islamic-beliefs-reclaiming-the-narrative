## Method

Walked the Persian source paragraph by paragraph against the English. The Persian extraction is heavily corrupted at the line/glyph level (RTL reordering, broken word-wrap, scrambled Arabic verse glyphs), but the running prose is recoverable. Arabic Quranic/hadith glyph scrambling was NOT flagged per instructions.

## Overall Assessment

The translation is **highly faithful and complete**. Every substantive Persian paragraph has a corresponding, full English rendering. The translator preserved the speaker's discursive, repetitive oral style (rhetorical questions, "this is one / this is two", asides to the audience) rather than condensing it. No paragraphs were dropped or summarized. All five footnoted Quran verses and all numbered hadith/lexical notes are carried over. Quranic verses are rendered with both Arabic and English gloss.
