## Minor observations (non-blocking, no fix required)

1. **src 776 `عُرضه‌اش کمتر است`** ("whose capacity/competence is less") is rendered
   in EN ¶77 as "a god whose power is less." The source actually says one god's
   *power* is greater (`قدرتش بیشتر`) and the other god's *ʿurḍa* (capacity/competence)
   is less — a slight lexical flattening of two near-synonyms into "power/power."
   Meaning is preserved; severity trivial. Optional fix: "...a god whose competence
   is less" for the second clause.

2. The verse-glyph scrambling in the Persian source (Arabic Quranic text) is an
   extraction artifact; the English correctly reconstructs the standard Quranic text
   and transliterations. Per instructions, not flagged as an error.
