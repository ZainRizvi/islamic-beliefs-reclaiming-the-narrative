## Minor findings

1. **[MINOR — style/precision] EN ¶7 "polity (madīnah)."**
   Source: `جامعـه‌ای و مدینـه‌ای ،مجتمعـی و نظامـی به‌وجود آورد`.
   The English renders the four nouns as "a society, a polity (madīnah), a community, and an order." Faithful; the gloss "(madīnah)" is an editorial addition (acceptable). No fix needed — noting only that the parenthetical is the translator's, not in the source.

2. **[MINOR — added emphasis] EN ¶1 closing: "in the next world a recompense, a reward, and a return."**
   Source: `در آخـرت اجـر و ثـواب ... و پـاداش بـه آنها ببخشـد`. Persian has اجر / ثواب / پاداش (reward/recompense/reward). The English "a recompense, a reward, and a return" is a fair three-fold rendering; "return" is a slight stylistic stretch for پاداش but not a meaning error.

3. **[MINOR — interpretive nuance] EN ¶12 "thought that they had been denied."**
   Source commentary (`خیال کردند که اشـتباه فهمیده‌اند... خدا چنین وعده‌ای به آنها نـداده بـوده`) supports "denied/lied-to" reading of *kudhibū*; the English handles the ambiguity well. No fix.

4. **[MINOR — completeness check, NOT an error] The mimeographed-handout footnote (Persian l. 183 `به پلی‌کپی انتهای جلسه مراجعه بفرمایید`)** is correctly carried as footnote 2 ("Refer to the mimeographed handout at the end of the session"). Confirmed present.
