## Minor notes (no fix required; not fidelity defects)

1. **(Polish, optional) Translator interpolation in supplication 8.** English line 157 renders «دشمنان سرسخت لجوج دین و مسلمین و همۀ بشر، یعنی یهود» as "the stubborn, obstinate enemies of religion and of the Muslims and of all humankind — namely the Jews **[of the Zionist enmity]**." The bracketed phrase has no counterpart in the Persian, which says only «یعنی یهود» ("namely the Jews"). This is a clearly-bracketed editorial gloss, not a mistranslation or omission, and arguably serves the reader. If strict source-fidelity is the priority, the bracket could be dropped or moved to a footnote; otherwise acceptable as a translator's clarification.

2. **(Verified, not an error) Verse-glyph scrambling.** Per instructions, the scrambled Arabic Qur'anic glyphs in the Persian source (e.g., lines 18–137, 1121–1379) are an extraction artifact. The English supplies correct, fully-vocalized renderings of every cited verse. Not flagged.

3. **(Verified) Ordering of the mosque aside.** The long parenthetical donation appeal sits in the source immediately before the second (detailed) recitation of the al-Raʿd verses (source ~1082–1119); the English preserves exactly this placement (line 119 before line 121). No reordering issue.
